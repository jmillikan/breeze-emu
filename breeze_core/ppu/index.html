Emulates the Picture Processing Unit.

We emulate the NTSC console, which has a refresh rate of 60 Hz and a screen resolution of 256x224 pixels by default.

Documentation mostly taken from http://emu-docs.org/Super%20NES/General/snesdoc.html and http://wiki.superfamicom.org/
